About the Book

GET OFF MY LAWN

THE PRESIDENTIAL MEMIOR

JAMES MARGHERIO

A Moving Introspective

In this intimate and resonant account of his seven years in the White House, James Margherio takes the reader inside the Oval Office, the Situation Room, and the restroom during the most pivotal moments of his presidency. From Missy Elliot's selection as Vice President to his decision, one year before the end of his second term, that he was "done with this sh*t", Margherio explains in thoughtful detail the personal and professional considerations that shaped his choices. He offers a wry yet honest account of his own goals, met and unmet, and a withering take on the body odor of his detractors and political adversaries.

A Riveting History

Gain a new understanding of world events as Margherio guides the reader thorugh his own perspective of the protracted trade war with Santa Claus, legalizing Mars dust, the Hobbiton raids, and his transformative Fortnite victory that forestalled a fourth world war. He examines the shifting political lanscape of his early years as a newly militarized Facebook sought representation in the UN, candidly relates navigating the physical devastation and moral upheaval of the Noodle Incident, questions his own decision to wear socks with sandals, and mourns the loss of his half-eaten ice cream cone.

Free Coupons

In addition to a selection of photographs by famed White House photographer Arlo Bokeh the reader will find 10 stunning coupons chosen by the former president himself. From 15% off one full price item at Michael's to a staggering $50 off your purchase of $200 or more from Best Buy, Margherio gives you insight into, and discounts for, his favorite shopping trips.
